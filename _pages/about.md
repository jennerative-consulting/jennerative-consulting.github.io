## About Jennerative Consulting LLC

Jennerative Consulting LLC is a data management consulting firm 
based in Lansing, Michigan, founded by Jennifer Quack.

We help modern businesses build data foundations that scale — 
from strategy to execution.

## About Jennifer Quack

Jennifer Quack is a Principal Consultant specializing in data 
management, data architecture, and data governance.

With deep expertise in modern data platforms including Microsoft 
Fabric, Azure, Snowflake, and Databricks, Jennifer helps 
organizations turn their data into decisions.

## Our Approach

We believe that great data management isn't just about technology 
— it's about building the right foundation for your business to 
grow, make better decisions, and operate with confidence.
